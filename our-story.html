Our Story

After successfully hosting the inaugural Zimbabwean Women World Wide Do Dubai business summit, we attracted the attention of women from other nationalities who wanted to replicate the experience for their own countries.


Thus Dubai became a strategic meeting point for women from home and inthe diaspora to connect, network, strategize & to build and develop their business for the global market!


The idea has since germinated and evolved into an international movement. After initially organizing themselves and meeting at country level, the women meet for an international conference every 2 years at global level.
